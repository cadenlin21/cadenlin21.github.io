About me

I'm Caden, and I'm a student at Columbia University double majoring in math and computer science. I'm currently working as a technical analyst for Growth & Commercial, a middle-market consulting firm, where I'm responsible for developing and validating data-driven, regression-based models to help advise multiple F500 companies and $1B+ PE firms. Last summer, I worked as a quantitative research intern in Columbia Business School's Decision, Risk, and Operations division, where I developed a mid-frequency trading strategy advised by LLM sentiment analysis of financial news. Our paper, which explores the feasibility of LLM-based trading strategies and their backtesting, will be published in the Journal of Financial Data Science in the winter of 2024. I will be working as a quantitative derivatives trading analyst in Morgan Stanley's Institutional Equity Division in the summer of 2024.

Academics aside, I was born and raised in San Jose, California, but I lived for three years in Bangkok, Thailand, and I'm now located in New York City attending Columbia.
